## Conflict Handling

If WHO and ECDC disagree:

1. Keep both snapshots.
2. Prefer WHO DON for global event summary when it is newer or more detailed.
3. Keep ECDC for EU/EEA risk and daily European page status.
4. Explain the difference in user-facing copy.
